My Journey

I have a year of experience with Java backend development, building solutions for a content management platform. Currently, I'm diving into the world of frontend, enhancing my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

My goal is to become a full-stack developer, capable of building comprehensive and efficient web solutions.

My hobbies and passions

A lifelong gamer, I found in programming the opportunity to create my own digital adventures. The combination of strategy, problem-solving, and competition inherent in gaming has shaped my thinking and provided me with a solid foundation for programming.

Programming offers me the opportunity to create a more innovative and technological future. With dedication and continuous effort, I hope to build solutions that make a difference and allow me to continue learning and growing as a professional.
